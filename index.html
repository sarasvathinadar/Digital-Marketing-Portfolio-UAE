Context & Challenge

Zoho is one of India's largest B2B SaaS companies, serving millions of businesses globally. As the digital marketing landscape shifted towards AI-driven search, Zoho needed to think beyond traditional SEO — towards AI platform visibility, LLM citations, and generative engine optimisation (GEO). I was brought in to own both the technical SEO foundation and build forward-looking AI-SEO frameworks.

SEO & Technical Execution
  • Executed on-page and off-page SEO across Zoho's product suite — keyword strategy, content optimisation, internal linking architecture, and backlink acquisition
  • Performed technical SEO audits covering Robots.txt, sitemaps, canonical tags, hreflang, mobile-first indexing, Core Web Vitals, page speed, and structured data/schema markup
  • Analysed GSC data for crawl anomaly detection, organic growth gap identification, and keyword ranking improvement strategies — converting insights into actionable prioritised fixes
  • Conducted competitive keyword gap analysis to identify untapped SERP opportunities across B2B SaaS categories
AI-SEO & GEO Framework — The Differentiator

As AI-powered search (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude) began reshaping how B2B buyers discover products, I built Zoho's first AI-SEO and GEO framework from scratch — a structured approach to ensuring Zoho's brand appeared in LLM-generated answers, not just traditional SERPs.

  • LLM visibility tracking: Mapped how Zoho's products appeared in responses across major AI platforms, identifying citation gaps vs. competitors
  • Brand citation strategy: Developed content and structured data optimisations to increase Zoho's citation frequency in AI-generated B2B software comparisons
  • AI-prompt analysis using Profound: Tracked which prompts triggered competitor mentions over Zoho, and built content to intercept those intent signals
  • GEO-optimised content briefs: Created a repeatable framework for writing content that AI models reliably retrieve and cite
ASO — Android & iOS
  • Acquired end-to-end ASO expertise for Zoho's Android and iOS apps — metadata optimisation, keyword targeting, A/B testing store listings, and performance tracking via MobileAction, Play Console, and App Store Connect
  • Applied conversion rate thinking to app store pages — treating store listings as landing pages with measurable conversion funnels
